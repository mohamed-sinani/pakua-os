DMCA Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

PakuaOS respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. In accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), we will respond expeditiously to claims of copyright infringement that are reported to us.

Filing a DMCA Notice

If you believe that content hosted on or linked through PakuaOS infringes your copyright, please send a DMCA notice containing the following:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent
  • Identification of the copyrighted work claimed to have been infringed
  • Identification of the material claimed to be infringing, with enough detail for us to locate it (URL of the page on our site)
  • Your contact information (name, address, telephone number, email)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information in the notice is accurate

How We Handle Claims

Upon receiving a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  • Promptly remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing material
  • Notify the user who posted the material, if applicable
  • Document the claim as required by the DMCA

Counter-Notification

If you believe your material was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification with us containing your statement under penalty of perjury and your consent to jurisdiction.

Important Note

PakuaOS links to official download sources — we do not host software files ourselves. If you are looking for a specific software download to be removed, please contact the original source directly as well.
