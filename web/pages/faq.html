Frequently Asked Questions

What is PakuaOS?

PakuaOS is a free tool that helps you find and download operating systems (Windows, Linux, macOS) and popular software applications. We aggregate verified download links from official sources.

Is PakuaOS free to use?

Yes, PakuaOS is 100% free. No registration, no subscriptions, no hidden fees. We are community-driven and supported by advertising.

Are the downloads safe?

We link directly to official sources whenever possible and verify checksums against published hashes. However, you should always scan downloaded files with your preferred antivirus software before installing.

Do you host the files?

No. PakuaOS does not host any software. We provide links to official download pages, trusted mirrors, and archive.org backups. The actual files come directly from the software vendors.

Why do some links use archive.org?

Sometimes official download links expire (especially for older versions). We provide archive.org mirrors as fallbacks so you can still access discontinued releases when needed.

Can I request a specific software or OS?

Absolutely! Use our Contact page to suggest new downloads. We regularly expand our curated lists based on user requests.

Do I need an account?

No account is needed. Simply search for what you need, and click download. Your download history is stored locally in your browser.

Which operating systems do you support?

We currently cover Windows (10, 11, Server), Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, Fedora, Arch, Kali, Mint, openSUSE, and more), and macOS (Sequoia, Sonoma, Ventura).

Is this project open source?

Yes. PakuaOS is built with PHP and Symfony Console and is open for community contributions. Check our GitHub repository for the source code.

How can I support PakuaOS?

You can support us by disabling your ad blocker on our site, sharing PakuaOS with others, contributing code, or contacting us with feedback and suggestions.
