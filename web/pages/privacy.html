Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

PakuaOS ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

Information We Collect

PakuaOS is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics (page views, referral sources) to understand how our site is used.
  • Contact Information: Only if you voluntarily provide it through our contact form.
  • Download History: Stored locally on your device — never sent to our servers.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not require account registration

  • We do not sell or share personal information with third parties

External Links

Our site contains links to external download sources (official websites, archive.org, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information we do collect. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

PakuaOS does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our services are intended for general audiences.

Changes to This Policy
